Control your computer from your phone

Control from anywhere
Control from anywhere, anytime.

By moving quick, commonly used actions to your phone, Dequeue prevents you from having to stop what you’re doing on your computer just to change the volume or play a new song. Whether you’re getting work done, playing a game, or out of the house, Dequeue is the perfect way to control your computer remotely.
The home screen is the app's main interface. It acts as a launching off point for the users to work with their computers.
Widgets can be found on the home screen, allowing the user to carry out more specialized actions with custom designed interfaces.
The actions page is all about the users automations.
From here, the user can create their own actions and workflows.
Carrying out those actions is as easy as a click of a button!
